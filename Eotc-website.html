Eastern Orthodox Christianity

A rich history and faith preserved through thousands of years.

Eastern Orthodox Christianity is a branch of Christianity that traces its roots to the early Christian Church and is centered in the Eastern Mediterranean region.

Church History Before the Bible

This goes deep into the church's history without the Bible, just the Old Testament. The church was there before the Bible was created. Despite only having the prophets and the Old Testament, the church was fruitful and multiplied abundantly.

The Orthodox Faith believes in the Nicene Creed, which is a clear statement of their faith overall.
